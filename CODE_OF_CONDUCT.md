# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want DNTB to be a friendly, low-drama place to collaborate. Be respectful, assume good intent, and make room for people who are learning.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and specific in reviews.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Give credit when building on someone else's work.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull requests; or block contributors when necessary to keep the project healthy.

If something feels off, contact a maintainer privately or open a concise issue if private contact is not available.
